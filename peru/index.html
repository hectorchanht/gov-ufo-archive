Peru's OIFAA — reactivated 2022 under the Air Force.

OIFAA (Oficina de Investigación de Fenómenos Aéreos Anómalos) is the Fuerza Aérea del Perú's UAP investigation office. Originally founded in 2001, deactivated, then reactivated in 2022 by FAP directive. Receives reports from pilots, ATC and the public; cross-correlates with radar / satellite data. Sources: fap.mil.pe.
