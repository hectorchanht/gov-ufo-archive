UK's MoD UFO Desk — closed for good, files all here.

The Ministry of Defence's UFO desk and its public hotline ran from 1950 to 2009. On closure the surviving files — 52,000+ pages across the AIR, DEFE and PREM record groups — were transferred to The National Archives at Kew in successive tranches between 2008 and 2017. All are digitised and free to view via Discovery. Reusable under the Open Government Licence v3. Source: TNA UFO Reports.
