Chile's civil-aviation UAP office — the only one of its kind.

SEFAA (Sección de Estudios de Fenómenos Aéreos Anómalos) — formerly CEFAA — is the world's only UAP unit embedded inside a civil aviation authority. Sitting within DGAC, it investigates anomalies reported by pilots, air traffic controllers, and the public, and publishes monthly resolved-case dispatches. Records reusable under Ley nº 20.285 (Acceso a la Información Pública).
