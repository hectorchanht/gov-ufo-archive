Records Related to Unidentified Flying Objects (UFOs) and Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAPs) at the National Archives: Presidential Library Holdings

The National Archives and Records Administration (NARA) has records in its custody across multiple Presidential Libraries related to unidentified flying object (UFO) and unidentified anomalous phenomena (UAP) investigations, sightings, and reporting. 

This page provides a list of presidential records related to UFOs and UAPs in the National Archives Catalog. The page is organized by Presidential Library, with links to featured content in the Catalog. This page will be updated as new records are made available online in the National Archives Catalog.

Click the link for each series or item to view descriptions of the records in the National Archives Catalog, as well as digital copies of our holdings, where available.
