All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office

AARO leads the U.S. government's effort to detect, identify, attribute, and mitigate Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena using a rigorous scientific framework and a data-driven approach. This is an offline archival mirror of aaro.mil — every video, image, and report you see below is served from this local archive.

§ Headlines · The mission, distilled
Mandate
Detect, track, attribute, and resolve UAP near national-security areas.
Established
2022
Stood up under the Office of the Under Secretary of Defense for Intelligence & Security.
Director
Dr. Jon T. Kosloski — formerly NSA Research Directorate; Ph.D., Electrical Engineering (Johns Hopkins).
Methodology
Rigorous scientific framework. Reports treated like intelligence: collected, analyzed, attributed.
Outputs
Case Resolution Reports · Historical Record Reports · Periodic Trend Reports · Public imagery.
Transparency
